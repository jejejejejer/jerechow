About Chowking

Chowking is a beloved Filipino-Chinese fast-food chain delivering comfort food made with love. We serve bold flavors and satisfying meals at affordable prices.

Founded in 1985, Chowking began with a dream to blend Chinese culinary heritage with Filipino taste. Over the years, we’ve become a household name with over 600 branches locally and abroad.

We’re known for iconic meals like Chao Fan, Siomai, Lauriat, and Halo-Halo. What sets us apart is our consistent commitment to authenticity, quality, and value – all delivered fast and fresh.
